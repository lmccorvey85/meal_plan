Apple-Banana Smoothie

Back to Meal Plan

Ingredients (1 serving)

  • 1 apple, diced
  • 1 banana
  • 1 Kroger Greek Yogurt Cup (5.3 oz)
  • ½ cup Kroger Milk
  • ½ tbsp honey

Instructions

  1. Blend apple, banana, yogurt, milk, and honey until smooth.
  2. Serve immediately.

Nutrition (per serving)

~220 kcal, 40g carbs, 8g protein, 3g fat, 5g fiber
