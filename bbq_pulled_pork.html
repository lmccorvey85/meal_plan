Southern BBQ Pulled Pork with Cornbread

Back to Meal Plan

Ingredients (5 portions)

  • Pork:
    • 6.5 lbs whole bone-in pork shoulder
    • 2 tbsp paprika
    • 2 tbsp garlic powder
    • 2 tbsp onion powder
    • 2 tsp chili powder
    • 2 tsp salt
    • 1 tsp black pepper
  • BBQ Sauce:
    • 1 cup ketchup
    • ½ cup sugar
    • ¼ cup apple cider vinegar
    • 2 tbsp honey
    • 2 tsp paprika
    • 1 tsp garlic powder
    • 1 tsp onion powder
    • 1 tsp black pepper
  • Rice:
    • 2 bags Success Boil-in-Bag Rice
  • Cornbread:
    • 1 cup Kroger Cornmeal
    • 1 cup all-purpose flour
    • 1 cup Kroger Milk
    • 1 egg
    • ¼ cup sugar
    • 2 tbsp Kroger Butter, melted
    • 1 tbsp baking powder
    • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Prepare Pork: Mix paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Rub over pork shoulder. Place in slow cooker, fat side up. Cook on high for 4–5 hours until tender. Remove, discard bone and fat, and shred (~4–4.5 lbs yield).
  2. Make BBQ Sauce: Combine ketchup, sugar, apple cider vinegar, honey, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, and black pepper in a saucepan. Simmer uncovered on low for 10–12 min, stirring, until thickened. Mix half with pork; reserve rest for serving.
  3. Cook Rice: Cook 2 rice bags per package instructions (boil 10 min). Drain.
  4. Make Cornbread: Preheat oven to 400°F. Mix cornmeal, flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Whisk milk, egg, and butter separately; combine with dry ingredients. Pour into greased 8x8 pan. Bake 20–25 min. Cut into 5 pieces.
  5. Serve: For Tuesday, serve 2 portions (~1¼ cups pork, ½ cup rice, 1 cornbread piece, drizzle of sauce each). Store 3 portions (same size) in fridge.
  6. For Leftovers: Reheat pork and rice in microwave (2–3 min), serve with cornbread.
  7. Note: Freeze extra pork (~0.5–1 lb) for up to 3 months.

Nutrition (per portion)

~630 kcal, 58g carbs, 38g protein, 27g fat, 4g fiber
