BBQ Chicken Bake with Corn and Rice (Leftover)

Back to Meal Plan

Details

Servings: 1
Prep Time: 2 min
Cook Time: 2–3 min (reheating)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup BBQ chicken bake mix (from dinner)
  • 1/2 cup cooked Success boil-in-bag rice

Cutting Instructions

  • None (pre-cooked).

Instructions

  1. Place 1 cup chicken bake mix and 1/2 cup rice in a microwave-safe container.
  2. Microwave on high for 2–3 min, stirring halfway, until heated through.
  3. Alternatively, reheat in an oven at 350°F for 10 min.
  4. Serve hot.

Nutrition per Serving

  • Calories: 450 kcal
  • Protein: 28g
  • Fat: 15g
  • Carbs: 50g
  • Fiber: 3g
  • Sugar: 18g
