Pork Chops with Sweet Potato Mash and Green Beans

Back to Meal Plan

Details

Servings: 5
Prep Time: 20 min
Cook Time: 30 min

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs bone-in pork chops (4 chops)
  • 2 large sweet potatoes (2 lbs)
  • 1 lb frozen green beans
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Cutting Instructions

  • Peel sweet potatoes and cut into 1-inch cubes.
  • Trim green beans if needed (frozen are typically pre-trimmed).

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Season pork chops with 1/2 tsp salt and 1/4 tsp pepper. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium; sear chops 4 min per side until golden.
  3. Transfer chops to a baking sheet; bake for 15 min until internal temperature reaches 145°F. Let rest 5 min.
  4. Meanwhile, place sweet potatoes in a pot with water to cover; boil 15 min until fork-tender. Drain and mash with butter, milk, garlic powder, 1/4 tsp salt, and 1/8 tsp pepper until smooth.
  5. Steam green beans in a steamer basket or microwave with 2 tbsp water for 10 min until tender. Season with 1/4 tsp salt and 1/8 tsp pepper.
  6. Serve 1 pork chop, 3/4 cup sweet potato mash, and 1/2 cup green beans per portion.
  7. Store leftovers in airtight containers; refrigerate for up to 4 days.

Nutrition per Serving

  • Calories: 500 kcal
  • Protein: 30g
  • Fat: 20g
  • Carbs: 50g
  • Fiber: 8g
  • Sugar: 10g
