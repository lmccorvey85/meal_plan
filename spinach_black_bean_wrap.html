Spinach and Black Bean Wrap

Back to Meal Plan

Ingredients (3 servings)

  • 3 Kroger Tortillas
  • 1 can (15 oz) Kroger Black Beans, drained and rinsed
  • 3 cups fresh spinach
  • ¾ cup Kroger Shredded Cheddar
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Mix black beans with chili powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Lay out a tortilla, add 1 cup spinach, ⅓ of the beans, and ¼ cup cheese.
  3. Roll tightly and serve or wrap for later.

Nutrition (per serving)

~350 kcal, 45g carbs, 15g protein, 12g fat, 8g fiber
