Chicken and Sausage Stir-Fry with Success Rice

Back to Meal Plan

Ingredients (6 servings)

  • 2.5 lbs boneless chicken breast
  • 1 lb Kroger Mild Italian Sausage
  • 2 bags Success Boil-in-Bag Rice (family-size, 6 cups cooked)
  • 2 cups Kroger Frozen Mixed Vegetables
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1/2 yellow onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 cup Kroger Soy Sauce
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Prep (10 min): Cook rice bags according to package (10 min in boiling water). Drain and set aside.
  2. Cutting Instructions: Cut chicken into 1-inch cubes. Slice sausage into 1/2-inch rounds. Slice bell pepper into 1/4-inch strips. Dice onion into 1/4-inch pieces. Mince garlic.
  3. In a large skillet, heat 1 tbsp oil over medium-high heat. Add sausage and cook until browned (5-7 min). Remove and set aside.
  4. Add remaining 1 tbsp oil to the skillet. Add chicken, 1/2 tsp salt, and 1/4 tsp pepper. Cook until browned (6-8 min).
  5. Add onion, garlic, bell pepper, and frozen vegetables. Cook until vegetables are tender (5 min).
  6. Return sausage to the skillet. Mix soy sauce, honey, remaining salt, and pepper. Pour over meat and vegetables, stirring to coat. Cook 2 min for sauce to thicken.
  7. Serve 2 portions over rice. Store 4 portions in airtight containers for leftovers.

Nutrition (Per Serving)

Approx: 460 kcal, 32g protein, 45g carbs, 16g fat, 3g fiber

Prep Time

10 min

Cook Time

18-23 min
