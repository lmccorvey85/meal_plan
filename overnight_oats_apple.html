Overnight Oats with Apple

Back to Meal Plan

Ingredients (1 serving)

  • ½ cup Kroger Old Fashioned Oats
  • ½ cup Kroger Milk
  • 1 apple, diced
  • ½ tbsp honey
  • ¼ tsp cinnamon (optional)

Instructions

  1. The night before, mix oats, milk, diced apple, honey, and cinnamon in a container.
  2. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
  3. In the morning, stir and eat cold or warm in the microwave (1 min).

Nutrition (per serving)

~260 kcal, 42g carbs, 8g protein, 4g fat, 6g fiber
