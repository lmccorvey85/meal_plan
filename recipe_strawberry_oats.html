Strawberry Trail Mix Overnight Oats

Back to Meal Plan

Details

Servings: 1
Prep Time: 5 min
Cook Time: 0 min (refrigerate overnight)

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Kroger Old Fashioned Oats
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup strawberries
  • 2 tbsp trail mix
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tsp honey
  • Pinch salt

Cutting Instructions

  • Slice strawberries into 1/8-inch slices.

Instructions

  1. In a jar, combine oats, milk, chia seeds, honey, and salt.
  2. Add strawberries and trail mix; stir well.
  3. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
  4. Stir before serving; eat cold.

Nutrition per Serving
