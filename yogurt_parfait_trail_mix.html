Yogurt Parfait with Trail Mix

Back to Meal Plan

Ingredients (1 serving)

  • 1 Kroger Greek Yogurt Cup (5.3 oz)
  • ¼ cup Kroger Trail Mix

Instructions

  1. The night before, portion ¼ cup trail mix into a container.
  2. In the morning, top yogurt cup with trail mix.

Nutrition (per serving)

~200 kcal, 20g carbs, 10g protein, 8g fat, 2g fiber
