Overnight Oats with Berries

Back to Meal Plan

Ingredients (4 servings)

  • 2 cups Kroger Old Fashioned Oats
  • 2 cups Kroger Milk
  • 1 cup Kroger Frozen Mixed Berries (thawed)
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional)

Instructions

  1. The night before, mix ½ cup oats, ½ cup milk, ¼ cup thawed berries, and ½ tbsp honey per serving in a container.
  2. Stir well, cover, and refrigerate overnight.
  3. In the morning, stir and eat cold or warm in the microwave (1 min).

Nutrition (per serving)

~250 kcal, 40g carbs, 8g protein, 4g fat, 5g fiber
