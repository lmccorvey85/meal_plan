Trail Mix and Fruit Salad with Yogurt

Back to Meal Plan

Details

Servings: 1
Prep Time: 5 min
Cook Time: 0 min

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup trail mix
  • 1/2 cup strawberries
  • 1/2 banana
  • 1/2 cup Kroger yogurt (6 oz)
  • Pinch salt

Cutting Instructions

  • Slice strawberries into 1/4-inch pieces.
  • Slice banana into 1/4-inch rounds.

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine trail mix, strawberries, banana, yogurt, and salt.
  2. Mix gently to combine.
  3. Serve immediately.

Nutrition per Serving

  • Calories: 380 kcal
  • Protein: 12g
  • Fat: 16g
  • Carbs: 50g
  • Fiber: 6g
  • Sugar: 30g
