Berry Banana Smoothie

Back to Meal Plan

Details

Servings: 1
Prep Time: 5 min
Cook Time: 0 min

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup Kroger yogurt (6 oz)
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tsp honey

Cutting Instructions

  • Peel banana (no cutting needed).

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine blueberries, banana, yogurt, milk, and honey.
  2. Blend on high for 30â€“60 seconds until smooth.
  3. Pour into a glass and serve immediately.
