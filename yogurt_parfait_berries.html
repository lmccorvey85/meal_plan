Yogurt Parfait with Berries

Back to Meal Plan

Ingredients (1 serving)

  • 1 Kroger Greek Yogurt Cup (5.3 oz)
  • ¼ cup Kroger Frozen Mixed Berries (thawed)

Instructions

  1. The night before, portion ¼ cup thawed berries into a container.
  2. In the morning, top yogurt cup with berries.

Nutrition (per serving)

~180 kcal, 20g carbs, 10g protein, 3g fat, 2g fiber
