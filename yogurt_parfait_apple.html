Yogurt Parfait with Apple

Back to Meal Plan

Ingredients (1 serving)

  • 1 Kroger Greek Yogurt Cup (5.3 oz)
  • 1 apple, diced

Instructions

  1. The night before, portion diced apple into a container.
  2. In the morning, top yogurt cup with apple.

Nutrition (per serving)

~180 kcal, 22g carbs, 10g protein, 3g fat, 3g fiber
