Southern Chicken and Rice Casserole

Back to Meal Plan

Ingredients (6 portions)

  • 5 lbs boneless chicken breast, cut into chunks
  • 2 bags Success Boil-in-Bag Rice
  • 2 bags (12 oz each) Kroger Frozen Green Beans
  • 1 bag (8 oz) Kroger Shredded Cheddar
  • 1¼ cups Kroger Milk
  • 3 tbsp Kroger Butter
  • 1¼ tbsp garlic powder
  • 1¼ tbsp onion powder
  • 1¼ tbsp paprika
  • 1¼ tsp chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Cook rice bags per package instructions (10 min in boiling water). Drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, melt butter, season chicken with garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Cook until browned (8–10 min).
  4. In a large baking dish, mix cooked rice, chicken, green beans, milk, and half the cheese. Top with remaining cheese.
  5. Bake 25–30 min until bubbly and golden.
  6. Divide into 6 portions (2 for Friday, 4 for leftovers). Each portion is ~1¾ cups. Store in fridge.
  7. For Leftovers: Reheat ~1¾ cups in microwave (2–3 min).

Nutrition (per portion)

~600 kcal, 50g carbs, 45g protein, 22g fat, 5g fiber
