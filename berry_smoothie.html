Berry Smoothie

Back to Meal Plan

Ingredients (2 servings)

  • 2 cups Kroger Frozen Mixed Berries
  • 2 Kroger Greek Yogurt Cups (5.3 oz each)
  • 1 cup Kroger Milk
  • 1 banana
  • 1 tbsp honey

Instructions

  1. Blend 1 cup berries, 1 yogurt cup, ½ cup milk, ½ banana, and ½ tbsp honey per serving until smooth.
  2. Serve immediately.

Nutrition (per serving)

~200 kcal, 35g carbs, 8g protein, 3g fat, 4g fiber
