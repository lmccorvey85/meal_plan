BBQ Chicken Bake with Corn and Rice

Back to Meal Plan

Details

Servings: 6
Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 40 min

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs boneless chicken breasts
  • 2 cups BBQ sauce
  • 1 can (15 oz) corn, drained
  • 1 onion
  • 1 family-size bag Success boil-in-bag rice (3 cups cooked)
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Cutting Instructions

  • Dice onion into 1/2-inch pieces.
  • Cut chicken into 1-inch cubes.

Instructions

  1. Cook rice per package instructions (boil bag in water for 10 min); set aside to yield 3 cups cooked.
  2. Preheat oven to 375Â°F.
  3. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Season chicken with 1/2 tsp salt and 1/4 tsp pepper; brown for 5 min, stirring occasionally.
  4. In a 9x13 baking dish, combine browned chicken, 2 cups BBQ sauce, drained corn, diced onion, garlic powder, remaining 1/2 tsp salt, and 1/4 tsp pepper. Stir to coat.
  5. Bake for 30 min, stirring halfway.
  6. Sprinkle cheese over the top; bake 5 min until melted.
  7. Serve 1 cup chicken mix with 1/2 cup rice per portion.
  8. Store leftovers in airtight containers; refrigerate for up to 4 days.

Nutrition per Serving

  • Calories: 450 kcal
  • Protein: 28g
  • Fat: 15g
  • Carbs: 50g
  • Fiber: 3g
  • Sugar: 18g
