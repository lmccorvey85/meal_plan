Pork Chops with Sweet Potato Mash and Green Beans (Leftover)

Back to Meal Plan

Details

Servings: 1
Prep Time: 2 min
Cook Time: 2–3 min (reheating)

Ingredients

  • 1 pork chop
  • 3/4 cup sweet potato mash
  • 1/2 cup green beans

Cutting Instructions

  • None (pre-cooked).

Instructions

  1. Place pork chop, sweet potato mash, and green beans in a microwave-safe container.
  2. Microwave on high for 2–3 min, stirring mash halfway, until heated through.
  3. Alternatively, reheat in an oven at 350°F for 10–12 min.
  4. Serve hot.

Nutrition per Serving

  • Calories: 500 kcal
  • Protein: 30g
  • Fat: 20g
  • Carbs: 50g
  • Fiber: 8g
  • Sugar: 10g
